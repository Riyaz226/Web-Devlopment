WELCOME TO SMR COLLEGE

Mohamed Riyaz College, was founded in 1951, an autonomous institution, affiliated to the University Of Kerala.
The Kerala college is administered by the Society of Mohamed Riyaz College, is established in a
sprawling land area of 87 acres, as a religious minority institution,with the primary objective of providing higher
education to the downtrodden and socially backward sections of the society in general and Other minority
in particular. The college is Re-accredited (3rd Cycle) with 'A' grade by NAAC. The college has been identified
as the "College with Potential for Excellence" by the UGC.
