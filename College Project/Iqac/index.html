PROFILE OF THE COLLEGE

⇒Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Mohamed Riyaz College was established on 04.02.2005 as a post accreditation quality sustenance measure for performance evaluation, assessment and accreditation and quality upgradation of the College. Since quality enhancement is a continuous process, the IQAC has become a part of our College system and works towards realising the goals of quality enhancement and sustenance. The IQAC makes a significant and meaningful contribution in the post accreditation phase of institutions.

OBJECTIVES:

⇒IQAC of the College facilitates the attainment of the following objectives.

⇒To focus on institutional functioning towards quality enhancement and facilitate internalization of the quality culture in the College.

⇒To the enhancement and integration among the various activities of the College and institutionalize the healthy practices.

⇒To provide a sound basis for decision making to improve the functioning of the College.

⇒To act as a change agent in the College.

⇒To better internal communication.

IQAC ACTIVITIES:

⇒Organizational Arrangements in Internal Quality Assurance Cell.

⇒To develop action plan for academic year

⇒ To interact with management for introducing new academic courses in college.

⇒Improving infrastructural facilities.

⇒To act as a change agent in the College.

⇒To better internal communication.

⇒ICT as Teaching-learning Process

⇒Interaction with parents

⇒Term-wise teaching plan and research activities.

⇒Training of Non-teaching Staff for Automation Process.

⇒Participatory Learning.

⇒Soft Skills / Employability Skills.

⇒Mentoring / Tutor-ward System.

⇒Student Orientation Activities.

⇒Accepting innovative ideas from faculties and students.

⇒ Documentation of the various programmes / activities of the institution leading improvement.

⇒Submission of the Annual Quality Assurance Report, UGC Progress Report, Triennial Inspection Commission Report and such other reports as may be decided from time to time.
